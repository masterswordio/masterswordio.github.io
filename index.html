1.Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 4 tsp
Eggs 4
Creme fraîche 1 tsp
Salt 2 pinches
Chives 1 pinch

Step 1: Put the butter and the eggs in the pan

Step 2: Put the pan on the stove with medium-high heat. Mix by sraping the bottom and side of the pan then bringing it on top, keep on doing this.

Step 3: After about 90 seconds, take the pan awany from the stove for 20 seconds then put it back on. Continue to scrape the sides and bring it up.

Step 4: After another 90 seconds on the stove, take the pan off and add the creme fraîche, salt, and chives, mix thoroughly.

  • Chives are optional
  • Creme fraîche can be switched with yogurt

2.Almond Flour Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 45-50 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Melted unsalted Butter 1 tsp
Eggs 4
Honey 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tbsp
Almond flour 1 cup
Cocoa powder 1/2 cup
Salt 1/2 tsp
Baking soda 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Preheat overn to 325 degrees F. Line a 9-inch glass pie plate with a circle of parchment paper. Grease the pan well with butter.

Step 2: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, honey and vanilla extract.

Step 3: Gradually whisk in the almond flour and cocoa powder.

Step 4: Finally, whisk in the salt and baking soda. The batter will be fairly thin.

Step 5: Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Bake until center is set, and a toothpick inserted comes out clean, 30-35 min.

Step 6: Cool the cake on a cooling rack until it reaches room temperature, about 30 min, then slice and serve. Keep the leftovers in the fridge, in an airtight container, for up to a week.

3.Chorizo & Mozzarella Gnocci Bake

  • Time: 35 min
  • Serves: 1-6
Ingredients Quantity
Mozzarella ball 125g
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Finely chopped onion 1
Crushed garlic cloves 2
Chorizo, diced 120g
Chopped tomatoes 800g
Granulated sugar 1 tsp
Fresh gnocci 600g
Torn basil A small bunch

Step 1: Cut the mozzarella ball into chunks.

Step 2: Heat the oil in a medium pan over a medium heat. Fry the onion and garlic for 8-10 mins until soft.

Step 3:Add the chorizo and fry for 5 mins more. Tip in the tomatoes and sugar, and season.

Step 4:Bring to a simmer, then add the gnocchi and cook for 8 mins, stirring often, until soft. Heat the grill to high.

Step 5:Stir ¾ of the mozzarella and most of the basil through the gnocchi. Divide the mixture between six ovenproof ramekins, or put in one baking dish.

Step 6:Top with the remaining mozzarella, then grill for 3 mins, or until the cheese is melted and golden. Season, scatter over the remaining basil and serve.

4.Chocolate Lava Cake

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 4
  • No egg, No oven
For the batter
Ingredients Quantity
Chilled water 1/2 cup
Oil 3 tbsp
Lemon juice 1 tsp
Powdered sugar 1/3 cup
Vanilla essence 1 tsp
Salt 1 pinch
Cocoa powder 2 generous tbsp
Flour 1/2 cup
Baking soda 1/2 tsp
Dark Chocolate 1 peice per person
For dusting mould
Bowls the size of your cake 4
Butter 1 tsp
Flour 2 tbsp

Step 1: In a mixing bowl, put the chilled water, oil, lemon juice, powdered sugar, and vanilla essence. Mix it well.

Step 2: Line the 4 bowls with butter and keep them in the fridge/freezer for later.

Step 3: In another mixing bowl, put salt, cocoa powder, flour, and baking soda. Mix thoroughly.

Step 4: After a bit, take the 4 buttered bowls out of the fridge/freezer. Dust each of them with flour by pouring the flour in one, shaking and turning the bowl around so some flour sticks, then move it to the next one and repeat. Put it back into the fridge/freezer.

Step 5: Mix the wet and dry ingredients together and take out the 4 bowls

Step 6: Put 2-3 spoons of batter into each bowl, then put the chococlate in, and put 1-2 more spoons of batter. After that, put the bowls into the freezer for 1 min

Step 7: Finally remove it from the freezer and steam it in a steamer for 4-5 min. Take it out of the bowls and serve warm.😉
